Our Current Project Needs

We are actively seeking data for the following high-priority research projects. See where your slides can make the biggest impact.

The Digital Lung Cancer Atlas (DLCA)

Description: This project aims to create a comprehensive map of morphological and biomarker expressions in Non-Small Cell Lung Cancer (NSCLC).

Intended Use: To train AI models for predicting patient prognosis and response to immunotherapy based on H&E slides.

Population: Adult patients (age 18+), all genders, diverse ancestries, with confirmed NSCLC diagnosis.

Biomarkers: PD-L1, EGFR, ALK, ROS1.

Tissue: Formalin-Fixed Paraffin-Embedded (FFPE) lung biopsy or resection specimens.

Disease: Non-Small Cell Lung Cancer (Adenocarcinoma and Squamous Cell Carcinoma).

Pancreatic Ductal Adenocarcinoma (PDAC) Staging

Description: A focused effort to differentiate high-grade from low-grade PDAC and identify subtle morphological features of early-stage disease.

Intended Use: Development of an AI-assisted grading tool to reduce inter-observer variability among pathologists.

Population: Patients with surgical resection for PDAC, with corresponding pathology reports.

Biomarkers: H&E, MUC1, MUC5AC, Ki-67.

Tissue: FFPE pancreatic tissue.

Disease: Pancreatic Ductal Adenocarcinoma (PDAC).

How to Contribute

We’ve designed our submission process to be secure, straightforward, and respectful of your time.

  1. 1

    Register Your Project

    Create a secure account on our contributor portal and provide a brief overview of the data you wish to share.

  2. 2

    De-identify & Prepare

    Follow our step-by-step guide to ensure all data is fully anonymized and compliant. We provide tools to help you securely remove all PHI.

  3. 3

    Secure Upload

    Use our high-speed, encrypted transfer client to upload your WSI files and metadata securely to our private cloud.

  4. 4

    Data Curation & QC

    Our curation team will perform a quality-control check and work with you to finalize the metadata and documentation.

Security, Privacy, and Ethics

We understand that WSI data is sensitive. Protecting it—and your trust—is our highest priority.

Full Anonymization

We require all data to be fully de-identified before upload. We provide protocols to help you securely remove all Patient Health Information (PHI).

Bank-Grade Security

Our platform uses end-to-end encryption for all data in transit and at rest. Our servers are HIPAA and GDPR compliant.

Ethical Oversight

All projects and data use requests are fully approved by an independent Institutional Review Board (IRB) or Ethics Committee.

Data Control

You and your institution remain the owners of your data. You specify the terms of use through clear, standardized data-sharing agreements.
